Eventos para grupos

El único lugar en Cancún que expresa el arte escénico y lo mejor de la interpretación artística internacional fusionada con la diversión inigualable de la vida nocturna.

Deja tu evento en manos de nuestros servicios especializados y personalizados requeridos para las diferentes necesidades de grupos y convenciones, incentivos y congresos. Groups and events

The only place in Cancún that shows art on stage and the best international artistic performances together with the amazing fun of Cancun´s night life.

Leave your event in our hands as we can give special services that will match exactly the needs and expectations of groups, conventions and congresses. Our group coordinators will assist you with a wide range of services from theme parties and integration activities to gala dinners.

We are conveniently located 20 minutes from the airport and Cancun´s downtown and just a few steps away from the main shopping malls and restaurants of the hotel zone.

Theatre characteristics

  • Up to 450 people capacity.
  • 200 dinner tables.
  • 3 private areas.
  • Credit card payment available (Visa, Mastercard and American Express).
  • 4 bars.
  • National unlimited drinks: Smirnoff Vodka, Bacardi rum, Ambhar Tequila, Jameson Whiskey, juices and sodas.
  • Transportation One way: Hotel – Backstage (previous arrangement).
